HIV/AIDS Information & Support

Important Notice

Early detection and treatment are crucial for managing HIV/AIDS. Our facility provides confidential testing and support services.

Prevention Measures

  • Safe Sex Practices

    Use of protection during sexual activity.

  • Regular Testing

    Getting tested regularly, especially if you're sexually active.

  • Pre-Exposure Prophylaxis (PrEP)

    Medication that can help prevent HIV infection.

Our Support Services

  • Confidential Testing

    Private and quick HIV testing services.

  • Counseling

    Professional support for those affected by HIV/AIDS.

  • Treatment Support

    Guidance and support for managing HIV/AIDS treatment.

Remember
  • HIV is manageable with proper medical care
  • Early detection improves treatment outcomes
  • Support is available - you're not alone
  • Confidentiality is always maintained
